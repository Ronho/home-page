3. App Usage Permissions

The app requires access rights to the documents and camera to capture and save images for coin detection.
Furthermore, the app requires access rights for the Internet in order to send the images to a system for evaluation. The evaluation of the images by a freely selectable backend is not part of the app itself.

4. Data Processing and Purpose

The app itself processes data locally on the user's system. Images can be sent to a freely selectable system for evaluation ("backend"), which is not part of the app itself.

5. Storage Duration

The deletion of the stored personal data takes place when you make the deletion of the app, or when the data is no longer necessary to fulfill the purpose pursued with the storage and if their storage is or becomes inadmissible for other legal reasons.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Right to Information

You have the right to request information about the stored personal data free of charge. Upon your request, the information can also be provided electronically.
You can request information about the data you have provided to us as part of the app via the contact address of the data protection officer. This overview contains all the data you have provided.

6.2 Correction, Deletion, Restriction and Revocation of Data Processing

You have the right to request the correction, deletion or restriction of personal data concerning you. However, if your data is deleted or blocked, you will no longer be able to use corresponding services. Your right to deletion may be excluded by statutory retention obligations.
You may also object to data processing that is to be carried out on the basis of a legitimate interest for reasons arising from your particular situation. If you object, we will no longer process your personal data unless compelling legitimate grounds for the processing can be demonstrated which override your interests, rights and freedoms, or the processing serves to pursue or defend legal claims.

6.3 Assertion of the Rights

You can exercise your rights by informally notifying the data protection officer.
In the event of complaints, you may also contact a competent supervisory authority for data protection, such as the State Commissioner for Data Protection of Baden-Württemberg.
